About Omega Design

A planning studio focused on comfort, ventilation, flow, and buildability.

Based in Tish, Begampur, Hooghly, Omega Design works with clients who want design clarity before construction begins. We study your plot, priorities, and budget to shape options that are practical and elegant.

  • Clear room-wise planning with thoughtful zoning
  • Facade and massing previews before final commitment
  • Interior suggestions that match your lifestyle
  • Step-by-step communication during design development
  • Documentation support for smoother approval coordination
