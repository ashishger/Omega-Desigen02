How We Work

Our Design Process

A clear, step-by-step approach that keeps decisions smooth, communication open, and outcomes aligned with your vision.

From first call to final drawing — every step explained.

We've refined our process to reduce confusion and make design feel easy for our clients.

Step 01

Initial Consultation

We start with a free discussion — in person, on call, or via WhatsApp. You tell us about your plot, family size, budget range, and what you're hoping to achieve.

  • Plot dimensions and location details
  • Family size and room requirements
  • Budget and timeline overview
  • Style preferences and references
01
Step 02

Site & Requirement Study

We study your plot dimensions, orientation, neighbourhood context, and local conditions to understand the constraints and opportunities before any design begins.

  • Plot orientation and sun path analysis
  • Access point and setback review
  • Ventilation and light opportunity mapping
  • Local bylaw awareness check
02
Step 03

Concept Planning

Initial layout directions are prepared — typically 2-3 concept options showing different approaches to flow, zoning, and room placement. You review and give feedback.

  • 2-3 initial concept floor plan options
  • Room size and placement exploration
  • Staircase and service zone options
  • Feedback session to align direction
03
Step 04

Design Development

The chosen concept is developed into a detailed floor plan with dimensions, room labels, and technical notes. 3D visualization and exterior design is prepared alongside.

  • Detailed dimensioned floor plan
  • 3D exterior visualization (if included)
  • Interior concept references
  • Revision rounds for refinement
04
Step 05

Final Delivery

Approved designs are packaged into a clean, organized file set ready for your contractor, engineer, or authority submission. We remain available for follow-up queries.

  • Complete drawing set in PDF format
  • High-res 3D renders if applicable
  • Authorization drawings if required
  • Post-delivery support availability
05

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Omega Design.

How long does a typical project take?+

A standard 2D floor plan takes 5-7 working days after the initial consultation. Projects including 3D visualization typically take 10-14 days. Timelines depend on revision rounds and how quickly feedback is provided.

Do you visit sites in person?+

Yes, for projects in Hooghly and nearby areas we can arrange site visits. For distant projects, we work from plot documents, photos, and detailed client briefings shared online or via WhatsApp.

How many revisions are included?+

Standard packages include 2 revision rounds after concept approval. Additional revisions can be accommodated based on the scope of changes. We discuss this clearly before starting.

Can you help with approval drawings?+

Yes. Our Authorisation Design Support service prepares structured drawing sets that align with local authority requirements. We coordinate on documentation to make the submission process smoother.

What information do I need to provide?+

Plot dimensions (length, width, shape), number of floors, number of rooms required, family size, and any specific preferences or constraints. A site photo and plot document are helpful but not always required to start.
