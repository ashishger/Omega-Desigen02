About Omega Design

A planning studio focused on comfort, ventilation, flow, and buildability — serving Bengal families since 2021.

Who We Are

Built from a passion for thoughtful residential design.

Omega Design was founded in 2021 in Tish, Begampur, Hooghly, with a single mission — to bring clarity, quality, and confidence to residential planning in West Bengal.

We work with families, plot owners, and builders who want to understand exactly what they're building before construction begins. Our team combines design sensibility with real-world construction knowledge to deliver solutions that look great and work even better.

From a simple 2D floor plan to a complete 3D visualization package, every project gets the same level of attention — thorough site study, careful space planning, and clear communication throughout.
